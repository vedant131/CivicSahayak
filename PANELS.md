# CivicSahayak — Panel Reference

## p-home: Dashboard
Quick access grid with 6 feature tiles, 4 stat counters, emergency action row.

## p-eligibility: Scheme Eligibility Engine
6-field filter (category, gender, income, education, disability, employment) against 23-scheme database. Results show ministry, benefit amount, match badge, direct apply link.

## p-documents: Document Guide
8 tabbed guides: Caste Certificate, Income Certificate, Domicile, Aadhaar-Bank Seeding, Bonafide, UDID, RTI Application, Minority Certificate. Each has step-by-step, checklist, tips, warnings.

## p-rights: Know Your Rights
6 expandable cards → modal with constitutional references:
- Fundamental Rights (Art. 12–35)
- Minority Rights (Art. 25–30)
- SC/ST Protections (Atrocities Act)
- Women's Rights (DV Act, POSH, 498A)
- Disability Rights (RPwD 2016)
- RTI Act 2005

## p-complaint: Complaint Wizard
5-step wizard: Issue type → Describe → Right portal → Documents → Summary + print.

## p-legal: Legal Guidance
FIR 5-stage timeline, NALSA free legal aid, 6 legal resource cards.

## p-grievance: Grievance Portals
12 portals rendered lazily: CPGRAMS, NHRC, NCW, NCM, NCSC, NCST, SHe-Box, NSP, Lokpal, CVC, NALSA, RTI Online.

## p-tracker: NSP Status Tracker
5-stage scholarship pipeline visual, rejection reason guides.

## p-helplines: Helplines
12 helplines with call buttons: 112, 14433, 181, 1098, 14567, 1800-11-0031, 1930, 15100, 1800-11-4000, 011-26178854, 0120-6619540, 1800-11-6090.

## p-ai: AI Assistant
Local KB chatbot with 14 topic clusters. No API calls. Response from keyword matching on KB array in data.js.
